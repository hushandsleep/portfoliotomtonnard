Discovery
Phase

A company is investing money into the development of apps that will run on their new blockchain.

They want a web-application that will let users make transactions to eachother.

1.

The big question

How do I create a user-friendly application that informs the user about drugwaste and gives instructions on how to react?

2.

Interviews

Various parties have been interviewed about their interactions with drugwaste. What was important is the cases where the interaction with drugwaste went wrong.

3.

Ideation

With the info aquired by the interviews, sketching and wireframing commenced to establish the main functionalities of the app.

4.

Prototyping the product

Prototypes were created and tested with the users from various target groups. Multiple rounds led to multiple iterations and refinement.

Evolution

Countless rounds of prototypes, user testing, and tech exploration helped to refine the finalised interface and product.

The following screenshots are low fidelity prototype sketches slowly becoming more concrete with design and colors implemented.

Finished Product

A graphical redesign has been applied to give the final product a more modern feel.
